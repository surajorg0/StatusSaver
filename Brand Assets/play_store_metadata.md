### Full Description
**StatusSaver** is your ultimate companion for managing WhatsApp statuses. Never lose a great photo or video status again!

**Key Features:**
*   **7-Day Automatic Archiving**: Statuses you view are automatically cached and kept for up to 7 days, even if they disappear from WhatsApp.
*   **Instant Download**: Save any image or video status to your phone's gallery with a single tap.
*   **Built-in Media Player**: Watch videos and preview images directly within the app in full-screen.
*   **Dark Mode Support**: A beautiful, premium interface that supports your system dark theme to save your eyes.
*   **Privacy Focused**: We use the official Android Storage Access Framework. Your data stays on your device and is never uploaded.
*   **Multi-Language Support**: English and Hindi support for permission explanations.

**How to Use:**
1. Open WhatsApp and view the status you want to save.
2. Open StatusSaver - your viewed statuses will appear automatically.
3. Tap the 'Save' icon to keep it forever in your gallery.

*Note: This app is an independent tool and is not affiliated with WhatsApp.*
